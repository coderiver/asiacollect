Professional consumer debt collection

AsiaCollect is Asaia's leading Consumer Debt Collection company

The core of our value proposition to clients is professional consumer debt collection. We offer our services on a contingency fee basis, meaning that we only make money when we deliver tangible recovery results to our clients.

Our services also include a wide range of bank outsourcing services related to the consumer lending process. Established in 2015 in Singapore, AsiaCollect is in the process of rolling out Operations Centers across Southeast Asia.

WHAT WE DO

AsiaCollect is the first company to bring the international professional standards of debt collection into the Asian marketplace.

1

We cover all stages
of the collection cycle

We offer our services on a contingency fee basis, meaning that we only make money when we deliver tangible recovery results to our clients. Our collection services cover all stages of the collection cycle and include call collection through our state-of-the-art contact centers, field collection, as well as legal collection.

2

We use highly
automated workflows

We pride ourselves on our know-how in the application of technology to bring superior efficiency to the debt collection process. Our workflows are highly automated, and leverage such sophisticated tools as auto-dialing, skip tracing, predictive analytics, and psychometric scripting, all delivered in a distributed enterprise architecture.

3

We are a socially
responsible company

Our collection activities are based on our internal Code of Conduct, which reflects European ethical standards of professional debt collection activity. All our processes are underpinned by strong quality control to ensure that our operators and agents communicate with the client strictly within the bounds of the existing regulatory frameworks and our Code of Conduct.
